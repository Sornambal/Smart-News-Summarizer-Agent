organizations are working to promote environmental awareness and support
international climate change initiatives. Notably, RTCC, a non-profit

as the World Resources Institute's Climate Change News Hub are providing
credible and science-based information on the evolving climate landscape,
offering data-driven insights to support a more sustainable and equitable
future. By facilitating informed decision-making and driving a just transition,
these efforts aim to reduce humanity's harmful impact on the planet and
mitigate the effects of climate change, ultimately working towards a net-zero
future.
