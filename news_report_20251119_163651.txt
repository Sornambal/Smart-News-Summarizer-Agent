EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**Executive Summary:**
A low-pressure system has emerged in the Bay of Bengal, south of Tamil Nadu,
and is anticipated to intensify into a cyclonic storm. According to the Indian
Meteorological Department, the system is expected to develop into a depression
within the next 36 hours, potentially forming in the southeast of Sri Lanka. If
the system further intensifies into a cyclone, it may pose a threat to the
coastal regions of Tamil Nadu, warranting close monitoring by weather
authorities to ensure timely preparedness and mitigate potential damage.
